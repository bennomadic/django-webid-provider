What's New in django-webid-provider 0.2.0
=========================================

- improved post installation processs for certificate installation.
- some support for chrome's lack of feedback during public key generation.
- dialog to avoid accidental cert duplication.
- certs can be named now.
- introduced multipart mime for certificate delivery (broken on chrome).
